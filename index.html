What's new

  • Online Classes in STANES SCHOOL CBSE

    An online class is a course conducted over the Internet. They are generally conducted through a learning management system, in which students can view their course syllabus and academic progress, as well as communicate with fellow students and their course instructor. We are conducting online classes via Google meet. Every student can understand in our online classes, to avoid eye strain, We have only 3 periods a day and 40 minutes break between each classes. So students can rest their eyes. We strongly instruct that students should not spend a day on screen.

  • CCA Activity

    When schools are open before this pandamic situation, We conducted CCA Activity Once a month. CCA Activity means activity done by students like drawing, a strong message to citizens, etc... But, We cannot Conduct this activities now, because the students have to come to school. Even we planned conduct during this pandamic situation in online classes. Now, the students are able to participate in CCA Activities and they are happy that CCA activities are even conducted in online classes.
